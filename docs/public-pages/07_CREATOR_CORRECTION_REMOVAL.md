# Correction and removal requests

Creators can request attribution corrections, excerpt corrections, record removal, or suppression of future records from the same source.

Base2026 is an independent research project. It should make public expert knowledge easier to search while preserving attribution, source links, and reasonable creator control.

---

## What to include

Email [offflinerpsy@gmail.com](mailto:offflinerpsy@gmail.com) with:

- the Base2026 page URL or original post URL;
- the creator handle;
- the requested action;
- a short reason;
- a contact email for follow-up.

## What may happen next

- Attribution may be corrected.
- A public excerpt may be corrected.
- A source record may be hidden or removed.
- A source can be added to a suppression list to avoid accidental re-import.

## Public boundary

Full third-party transcripts are not published as standalone public pages by default.

The public site keeps attribution, source links, short evidence context, methodology, and a correction/removal path visible.
