A source library for creator video knowledge.

Base2026 is an independent research product by Alex Yarosh. It turns public short-form expert videos into searchable, attributed source records for SEO, GEO, AEO and AI-search research. It does not re-host creator videos, replace creators, or package this work as a client campaign.
